privacy policy

privacy for wiselock

Last updated: March 26, 2026. This policy is a starter version for the public site and should be reviewed before App Store release to make sure it matches your final data practices.

Overview

wiselock is a productivity app designed to help people reduce distraction by adding a short learning step before opening selected apps. We aim to collect as little personal information as possible while providing the core product experience.

Information the app may use

  • Your selected app restrictions and in-app preferences, which are used to provide the product experience.
  • Subscription and purchase status handled through Apple and RevenueCat so premium access can be managed.
  • Information you choose to send directly to us by email for support.

Family Controls

wiselock uses Apple Family Controls and related Screen Time technologies only after the user explicitly authorizes the app. These permissions are used to support user-selected app restrictions and the in-app shield experience. wiselock is intended for self-directed focus and productivity features, not covert monitoring.

Third-party services

wiselock may rely on Apple services and RevenueCat for purchases, subscriptions, and related account status. Those providers may process information according to their own policies.

How information is used

  • To power app blocking, shield presentation, and short learning flows inside the app.
  • To restore purchases and determine premium access.
  • To respond to support requests and improve the app experience.
